Let's go to Karimunjawa

The archipelago consists mostly of pre-Tertiary continental islands composed mostly of quartzite and shale covered by basaltic lava. Geologically, the islands are part of Sundaland. The islands have extensive and patchy coral reefs. The best time to visit the islands is during the dry season, usually from April to October.

Our Story

Karimunjawa Island, or commonly known as Karimunjawa is a hidden paradise located in Central Java, Indonesia. This place is crowded with tourists because of the beautiful beaches that are very tempting. In fact, at this time Karimunjawa Island is also named as the 5 most beautiful places in Indonesia because of the beauty of marine life, along with the beauty of its beaches. For those of you who like the atmosphere of the beach, but are bored with the atmosphere in Bali and other beaches, you can try to visit this place. Here, you can santai a beautiful beach atmosphere with fresh sea air in Karimunjawa. In addition, the number of tourists who come is still not too many, so you can feel the beach atmosphere more privately.
